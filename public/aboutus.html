About MOM

Your trusted companion for personalized postpartum care

Our Story

MOM was founded with a simple yet powerful mission: to ensure no mother feels alone during her postpartum journey. We recognized that the transition to motherhood, while beautiful, can also be overwhelming and isolating.

Our platform combines cutting-edge AI technology with evidence-based maternal health practices to provide personalized, accessible support 24/7. We believe every mother deserves expert guidance, emotional support, and reliable information during this crucial time.

From physical recovery to emotional wellness, from newborn care to self-care, MOM is here to guide, support, and empower mothers every step of the way.

Our Mission & Values

To empower every mother with the knowledge, support, and confidence she needs to thrive during her postpartum journey, creating a world where no mother feels alone or unprepared.

Compassion

We approach every interaction with empathy, understanding, and genuine care for mothers' well-being.

Safety First

All our guidance is based on current medical evidence and best practices in maternal health.

Community

We foster a supportive community where mothers can connect, share, and learn from each other.
